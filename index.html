📖 Grand Line Glossary
☠️ How to Play
One Piece Draft — complete guide
1
⚔️ Choose Your Opponent
Decide who you're facing. vs AI lets you draft solo — the computer picks for the opposing crew using a scenario-aware strategy. vs Player puts two humans at the same screen, trading picks in real time.
2
🏴‍☠️ Choose a Draft Mode
Two modes, two completely different strategies:
♾️ Unlimited Draft — Pick any 6 characters from the shared pool. No budget, pure decision-making. Every character is available.

💰 Budget Draft — You have 5,000₿ and a pool of 20 characters. Stronger characters cost more Berri — spend wisely or you'll run out of room.
S Tier — 1,800₿ A Tier — 900₿ B Tier — 450₿ C Tier — 200₿
3
🗺️ Select a Battleground
Choose from 10 iconic arcs — Alabasta to Marineford. Each battleground demands different crew skills. A navigation-heavy arc punishes combat-only teams; a combat-heavy arc exposes strategists. Study the demand bars before locking in.
🎲 Use Randomize for a surprise arc — great for keeping veteran players off their script.
4
🪙 The Coin Toss
Before the draft begins, a coin flip decides who picks first. The first pick is a genuine advantage — but the snake order is designed to balance it out by giving the second player two consecutive picks immediately after.
5
🐍 The Snake Draft
12 total picks — 6 per player — in a snake order. The direction reverses after each end so no player ever picks more than twice in a row.

6
ℹ️ Inspecting Characters
Every card shows a scenario fit score — how well that character's stats match the active battleground. Tap the i button on any card to open a full stat breakdown with all 6 attributes before committing to a pick.
⚔️ Combat ✨ Haki 🧠 Strategy 👑 Leadership 🧭 Navigation 🛡️ Support
7
⚡ Synergies
Certain character combinations unlock synergy bonuses that are applied to your final score. Some boost multiple stats at once. Others have trade-offs — the Monster Trio rockets combat but tanks strategy. There are 28 synergies in total. Check Glossary → Synergies to plan ahead.
📊
How Scores Are Calculated
After both crews are locked in, a 5-stage reveal builds each player's final percentage:
1
👥 Crew Power — raw stat match against the battleground's weighted demands
2
✨ After Bonuses — role diversity, Devil Fruits, Haki depth, synergy combos
3
🌊 Battleground Deduction — difficulty modifier × crew mismatch penalty for wrong stat coverage
4
☠️ Boss Threat — penalty if your crew's average falls below the boss's stat requirements
5
⚡ Final Score — the higher percentage wins. Tied scores go to the more rounded crew
💡
Pro Tips
🗺️ Draft for the battleground, not just raw power. A well-tuned average crew beats a powerful but mismatched one every time.

☠️ Check the boss before you draft. Every arc has a boss with specific stat requirements. Failing those tests costs you heavily at Stage 4. See Glossary → Bosses for full details.

⚡ Hunt synergies strategically. A single well-timed synergy pick can swing the score by several points — but telegraphing your combo lets the opponent block it.

🧭 Don't neglect support and navigation roles. They unlock diversity bonuses, fill synergy slots, and cover gaps that pure fighters leave wide open.
